Frequently asked questions

How does billing work?

Billing is monthly by active user. You can view invoices and usage from the billing console and upgrade/downgrade at any time.

Is my data encrypted?

Yes. Files are encrypted on-device and at rest. You can also enable customer-managed keys for additional control.

Do you offer SSO?

SSO, SCIM provisioning, and advanced admin controls are available on the Business and Enterprise plans.
