Frequently asked questions

How long does deployment take?
Most customers are collecting telemetry within 48 hours. Full enterprise rollout varies with scope; we provide a rollout plan and SOC onboarding to accelerate timelines.
Do you provide managed SOC services?
Yes. Our managed SOC operates 24/7 with tiered response options, playbooks, and integration into your ticketing and SIEM platforms.
How do you handle compliance?
We collect and retain audit logs, provide evidence exports, and ship compliance-ready reports for frameworks like SOC2 and ISO27001.
What integrations are supported?
We support cloud providers (AWS, Azure, GCP), common SIEMs, ticketing systems, and offer a robust API for custom integrations.
