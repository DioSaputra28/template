Frequently asked questions

How do I migrate tasks from another tool?
Import via CSV or connect through our integrations for a guided migration. Our team can assist with larger imports.
Is there SSO for teams?
Yes — team and enterprise plans support SAML and SCIM provisioning for user management.
What about privacy and data ownership?
You retain ownership of your data. We provide export tools and a clear privacy policy.
