Frequently asked questions

How long does onboarding take?
Typical onboarding is 2-6 weeks depending on telemetry and integrations. Our onboarding team handles ingestion, tuning, and a readiness review.
Do you support cloud platforms?
Yes. We ingest telemetry from major cloud providers, containers, and SaaS apps. We also provide managed detection for hybrid environments.
What are your SLAs?
We offer SLA-backed response times for incidents and regular reporting. Specifics vary by plan; contact sales for a service-level addendum.
Can we keep data on-prem?
We provide hybrid options including on-prem collectors and private cloud deployments for customers with strict data residency requirements.
