Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about how we work and what to expect.

How long is a typical engagement?
Engagements range from 4-week discovery sprints to multi-month product phases tailored to scope and outcomes.
Do you work with remote teams?
Yes. We collaborate with distributed teams using Figma, Notion, and regular syncs to keep momentum.
How do you handle IP and ownership?
We transfer ownership of final assets and provide clear licensing in our agreements to ensure you own the outputs.
Can you integrate with our engineering team?
Absolutely — we design with engineers in mind, supply tokens, and collaborate on component implementation when needed.
