Frequently asked questions

Do I need equipment?

No. Our coaches design plans for bodyweight or gym-based training depending on your access and goals.

Can I switch coaches?

Yes. You can request a different coach at any time without losing your history or progress tracking.

What if I get injured?

Coaches provide exercise regressions and recovery plans; they instruct you to pause or modify when necessary.

Do you offer team plans?

Yes. We provide enterprise options with coach staffing, analytics, and single sign-on.
