Frequently asked questions

Common questions answered simply.

Is my bank data safe?
We use read-only bank connections and bank-grade encryption. Your credentials are never stored on our servers.
Can I export my data?
Yes — export CSV or PDF reports for any date range from the app.
Do you support multiple currencies?
We support major currencies and automatic conversions in reports. Settings allow you to choose a base currency.
