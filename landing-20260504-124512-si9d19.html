Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about ArenaLink.

Can I integrate with my Twitch stream?

Yes. ArenaLink includes native Twitch authentication and overlays for match info and viewer interaction.

Is my data secure?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Enterprise plans include additional compliance options.

Do you offer SSO for teams?

Enterprise customers can enable SAML/SSO and private network options for single-tenant deployments.

Can I self-host?

Self-hosting and on-premises options are available for enterprise customers. Contact sales for details.
