How long does planning typically take?

Can you accommodate dietary and medical needs?
Yes — we coordinate directly with hotels and local partners to ensure appropriate arrangements for any dietary restrictions or medical requirements.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellation terms vary by supplier. We provide full transparency at booking and will advise on refunds and alternatives if plans change.
