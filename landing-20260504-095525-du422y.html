Frequently Asked Questions

If you have other questions, our team is available to assist.

What destinations do you specialize in?

We craft trips worldwide with deep expertise in Europe, the Mediterranean, Asia and remote islands. Recommendations are tailored to your interests.

How far in advance should I book?

For peak season travel we recommend 4-6 months lead time. For exclusive events and yachts, earlier planning provides the best availability.

Do you handle group travel?

Yes — we plan family gatherings, small private groups and corporate retreats with bespoke programming and logistics.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation terms vary by supplier; we aim to secure refundable options when possible and will outline terms during booking.
