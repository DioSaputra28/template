FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes. Please note dietary restrictions when booking and our team will work with the chef to accommodate.

Is there parking available?

Valet service is available on weekends. Street parking and nearby lots are also options.

Can I host private events?

Yes. We offer customizable private dining experiences for small groups to large parties.

What is your cancellation policy?

Reservations may be canceled up to 24 hours in advance for a full refund of deposits.
