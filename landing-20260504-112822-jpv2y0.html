Frequently asked questions

Is my data safe?

We use bank-level encryption and read-only integrations. We never store your bank credentials.

Which banks are supported?

We support thousands of institutions through secure partners. If yours is missing, contact support and we’ll add it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Billing is monthly and you can cancel from your account settings at any time.

Do you offer family plans?

Family plans are available for teams and households — contact sales for larger accounts and custom pricing.
