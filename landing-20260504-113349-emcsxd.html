Frequently asked questions

What does onboarding look like?
Onboarding is a staged process — initial discovery, deployment, tuning period, and then handover to our SOC. Typical completion is 2-6 weeks depending on scope.
Do you support hybrid cloud environments?
Yes. SentinelShield supports public cloud providers, containers, and on-prem systems through a combination of agent and API integrations.
What compliance frameworks do you help with?
We help customers prepare for and maintain ISO 27001, SOC2, PCI-DSS, and other industry-specific requirements using automated evidence collection and documentation.
How is data privacy handled?
We collect only telemetry necessary for detection and response. Data residency and retention policies can be customized per contract.
