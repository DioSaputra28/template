Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about reservations, accessibility, and events.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Yes — please note any allergies or preferences when reserving and our chef will tailor the menu where possible.
Is there on-site parking?
We offer validated street parking nearby and recommend rideshare for opening nights when availability is limited.
Can we host private events?
Yes. Contact our events team for sample menus and availability. We accommodate seated and standing receptions.
What is your cancellation policy?
Standard reservations can be canceled up to 24 hours in advance. Large party deposits vary by event size.
