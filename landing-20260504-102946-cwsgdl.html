Frequently asked questions

How do refills work?

Choose a plan or single refill. We'll send a refill pouch or bottle at the cadence you select. Return or recycle used containers with our prepaid mailers.

Are products safe for sensitive skin?

Yes — our formulas are dermatologically tested and free from harsh sulfates and parabens. Full ingredient lists are provided on each product page.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Pause, skip, or cancel your subscription from your account dashboard without fees.

Do you ship internationally?

We ship to most countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by region — check the shipping page for full details.
