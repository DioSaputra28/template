Yes — roles, filters, and audit logs are built-in. Enterprise plans include advanced moderation workflows.

Can I monetize events?

Creators can sell tickets, create subscriptions, and configure member tiers directly in the hub.

Is there an API?

Yes — REST APIs for automation, webhooks for events, and integrations for common tools.
