Frequently Asked Questions

Can I migrate from other platforms?

Yes. We provide migration tools and dedicated support for community data and roles.

We support OAuth, webhooks, and SDKs for major engines and streaming services.

What security features are included?

Role-based permissions, 2FA for admins, audit logs, and encrypted backups are available on Pro and Enterprise plans.

Yes. Private brackets, invites, and spectator controls let you run closed competitions.
