Frequently asked questions

Clear answers to common questions about our service.

What makes Atlas Luxe different?
We focus on privacy, personalized access and small group or private experiences curated with local partners and our network.
How far in advance should I book?
For peak seasons and exclusive villas, 6–12 months is recommended. For shorter getaways, 4–8 weeks is often sufficient.
Do you handle visas and travel documents?
We provide guidance and can coordinate with visa specialists, but clients are responsible for final submissions where required.
What is your cancellation policy?
Policies vary by supplier. We share clear terms during booking and work to protect client deposits when possible.
