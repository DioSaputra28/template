Frequently asked questions

Short answers to common questions about service, booking and cancellations.

How do you price bespoke trips?
We price based on itinerary scope, seasonality and included services. For many trips we provide a clear options sheet with line-item pricing.
Can you accommodate dietary or accessibility needs?
Yes. Our team coordinates dietary requests, mobility assistance and bespoke arrangements in advance with on-the-ground partners.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellation terms vary by supplier. We negotiate flexible terms where possible and always communicate fees during booking.
