Frequently asked questions

Are your materials really recycled?

Yes — we publish the material breakdown and partner certifications for each product on its detail page.

How do returns work?

We provide prepaid return labels for defective or end-of-life returns. Reusable parts are refurbished when possible.

Do you offset shipping emissions?

We offset a portion of our shipping emissions and continuously optimize our logistics partners for lower impact.

Can I get wholesale for retailers?

Yes. Visit our wholesale page or contact sales for volume pricing and retail support.
