Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For peak seasons and exclusive access experiences, we recommend booking 4–8 months in advance. We can still design exceptional last-minute travel when feasible.

Do you handle group travel?

Yes. From intimate family gatherings to private corporate retreats, we plan logistics and bespoke programming for groups of any size.

Can you manage special dietary requirements?

Absolutely. We coordinate with chefs, restaurants, and service providers to ensure all dietary and medical needs are met.

What is your cancellation policy?

Policies vary by component. We'll outline terms clearly in your proposal and manage any change requests on your behalf.
