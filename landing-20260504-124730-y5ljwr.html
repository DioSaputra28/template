Frequently asked questions

How does billing work?
Billing is monthly based on provisioned storage and outbound transfer. You can set caps and alerts from the dashboard.
Can we bring our own keys?
Yes. Bring-your-own-keys (BYOK) is available on Business and Enterprise plans with customer-managed rotation.
Is there an SLA?
We provide a 99.99% uptime SLA for Business and Enterprise customers with incident credits for downtime.
How do we migrate data?
We provide tools and hands-on migration support. Small datasets can be transferred via the dashboard; larger migrations are coordinated with our team.
