Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about seating, menu, and accessibility.

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes. Our chef prepares vegetarian and vegan options upon request. Please indicate dietary needs when reserving.

Is there street parking nearby?

Limited street parking is available. We recommend ride-share or nearby public parking for opening nights.

Can we host private events?

Yes. We offer private dining packages for groups. Contact our events manager for options and availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations within 24 hours of the reservation may be subject to a fee. Please review terms when booking.
