Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept walk-ins?
We recommend reservations for opening weeks; limited walk-in seating may be available depending on capacity.
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Yes. Please indicate allergies or dietary needs when booking and our team will do our best to accommodate.
Is there on-site parking?
We offer limited street parking; several public lots are within a short walk. Valet is not available during opening month.
