Frequently asked questions

How secure is my data?
All data is encrypted at rest with customer keys available on higher tiers. Network transport uses TLS 1.3.
What regions do you operate in?
Nimbus runs in multiple regions. You can select your primary region for data residency during onboarding.
Do you offer an SLA?
Yes. Enterprise customers receive a signed SLA with uptime credits and guaranteed support response times.
